🎓 MSc Thesis · RUET · Enrolled April 2026
Rooftop Solar Photovoltaic-Integrated Hybrid AC/DC Microgrid with Adaptive Grid-Forming and Grid-Following Control
Md. Mofrat Shahriar · Supervisor: Dr. Md. Sohel Rana · RUET EEE Department
MSc Research in Progress · Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology · 2026–Present

This research investigates the integration of rooftop solar PV into hybrid AC/DC microgrid architectures, developing adaptive control strategies that seamlessly transition between grid-forming (islanded) and grid-following (grid-connected) modes. The work addresses the control challenges arising from high penetration of inverter-based resources in weak distribution grids, targeting real rural Bangladesh grid conditions.

📝 Conference Paper · In Preparation
Impact of Generation Mix Transition, Load Shedding, and Cross-Border Import Dependency on Power Demand Forecasting Accuracy
Md. Mofrat Shahriar et al.
Target: IEEE Transactions on Power Systems / Sustainable Energy, Grids and Networks · 2025–2026

Investigates how structural changes in Bangladesh's generation mix (coal, gas, renewables, oil), periodic load shedding events, and growing dependency on cross-border power imports from India systematically distort short-term electrical demand forecasting models. Develops correction frameworks that account for these structural non-stationarities.
